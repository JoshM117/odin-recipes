Hotdogs

Description

This is a recipe for hotdogs. Hotdogs are easy to make and very delicious. They can be cooked in under 30 mins. Here is everything you need to get started.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog Buns
  • Hotdogs
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cook hotdogs on stove
  2. Put hotdog in bun
  3. Put mustard on hotdog
  4. Put ketchup on hotdog
  5. Enjoy!
