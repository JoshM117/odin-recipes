Taco

Description

This is a recipe for tacos.Tacos are easy to make and very delicious. They can be cooked in under 30 mins. Here is everything you need to get started.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Taco meat
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook taco meat on stove
  2. Put taco seasoning on taco meat
  3. Put taco meat in shell
  4. Put lettuce in shell
  5. Put cheese in shell
  6. Enjoy!
